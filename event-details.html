FAQs

Questions we hear the most.

Can't find what you're looking for? Email us directly.

What's included in the price?+

Everything under The Investment above is included in one price — supercar access, hotels, meals, transfers, ferries, fuel, insurance and support crew. The only things you need to bring are yourself and a good attitude.

Do I need a special license or experience to drive?+

You'll need a valid driving license held for at least three years and a comfortable, controlled driving style. This isn't a track day — no racing experience required, just confidence behind the wheel on real roads.

What if I don't want to drive the whole time?+

Every guest rotates through the fleet, so no one is stuck behind the wheel — or out of it — longer than they'd like. Non-driving partners are welcome and have a full seat at every meal, hotel and experience.

What happens if the weather turns?+

The Dalmatian Coast sees very little rain through the summer season, but the itinerary flexes around weather where needed. Hotels, meals and ferries are unaffected either way.

Is travel insurance included?+

Vehicle insurance is included for the driving. We recommend guests arrange their own personal travel insurance for flights and medical cover.

What's the payment & cancellation policy?+

A deposit secures your spot for your chosen departure, with the balance due ahead of the event. Full terms are confirmed directly with our team — email us and we'll walk you through it.

How many people join each departure?+

Six guests per event, keeping the group small, social and easy to coordinate across hotels, restaurants and the fleet.
