Why This Exists

We built the trip we'd want to book ourselves.

Spirited Drives is run by a small team of car people who wanted an event that didn't already exist: a fully-hosted, multi-day driving weekend built around real coastal roads, not a track day or a bring-your-own-car rally.

We picked the Dalmatian Coast because it's some of the best driving we've ever found, and because chasing good roads in beautiful places is what got us into cars in the first place. This is the trip we would have dreamed up as kids: a proper bucket list drive, in the cars we always wanted to get behind the wheel of. We're just building the version of it we always wished existed, and inviting people who feel the same way to come along.

The Route

Friday night to Monday morning.

A weekend across Split, Brač and Hvar. The full route is mapped in advance.

01
FridayArrival, Split

Arrive into Split and transfer to the hotel. A team dinner and route briefing, then time to explore the city before the driving begins.

Team BriefingWelcome Dinner
02
SaturdayBrač & The Coast Road South

Early crossing to Brač for a morning spent driving the island's back roads and coastal scenery, then back to the mainland and south along the Dalmatian coast to Omiš for the night.

Island B-RoadsCoastal SceneryDrive to Omiš
03
SundayThe Canyon & Hvar

A canyon drive out of Omiš, then south along the coast to Hvar. The afternoon brings a stop at a winery, followed by a beach club and dinner on the water.

Canyon DriveWineryBeach Club
04
MondayDeparture

Back to Split, keys returned, and goodbyes before the transfer to the airport.

Final FarewellsAirport Transfer
